Are You Hungry, but would you like to know about us?

Juan’s Mexican Street Food was founded in 2021 by Luisa Nunes, IT student from NCI, spurred on by an infatuation with Mexico and its amazing cuisine gained during her youthful travels to the Americas.
After a few false starts, she launched what is now Juan’s Mexican Street Food Bar in Rathmines, inspired by the flavours, aromas and laid back vibes of Mexican street food.
In November (2021), Luisa moved on from Juan’s Mexican Street Food, but her dedication to quality authentic ingredients, traditional recipes and cooking methods lives on strong. Principals hard-woven into the fabric of who we are and the amazing Mexican food we make fresh every day.
Juan’s Mexican Street Food, simple Mexican inspired restaurant business, that uses local ingredients, where possible, made fresh daily and served in a fresh, friendly and vibrant atmosphere.
